About Us

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Anjuman Babul Hawaej(ABH) work in the small village of Ghavis is a testament to their commitment to improving education and the overall well-being of underserved communities. In Ghavis, the organization has specifically tailored its programs to address the unique needs of this village and its residents
Our mission at AnjumanBabulHawaej(ABH) is to transform the lives of the underserved village of Ghavis by nurturing hope, building dreams, and creating a brighter future. We achieve this through a holistic approach, providing education support with scholarships, school supplies, and vocational training, ensuring access to healthcare services, and engaging in social development by enhancing livelihoods, improving infrastructure, and involving the local community in decision-making. Our mission is to uplift and empower the community of Ghavis, breaking the cycle of poverty and fostering a sense of self-worth.
Our vision at AnjumanBabulHawaej(ABH) is to see the underserved village of Ghavis transformed into a thriving and empowered community. We envision a future where every child in Ghavis has equal access to quality education, where healthcare is readily available, and where social development initiatives have improved livelihoods and infrastructure. Our vision is one where hope and dreams are nurtured, and where the people of Ghavis have the tools and opportunities to build a brighter future, breaking the cycle of poverty and creating a community of self-reliance and empowerment.

Prize Money Distribution

Anjuman Babul Hawaij held a prize distribution ceremony to award talented students for their accomplishments. The event was an inspiration to all who attended, and it was a reminder of the importance of education and the power of talent

Water Supply Projects

ABH, a non-profit, they helped in providing of water(actually provided by a politian) pipe to Ghavis village, which previously relied on water pipe and other temporary methods. This will provide villagers with reliable access to clean water

Prize Distribution

Students in Ghavis village celebrated the prize distribution ceremony organized by Anjumanbabulhawaej. Students were thrilled to receive prizes for their academic achievements and for their contributions to the community.

Free Winter Camp

Anjumanbabulhawaej free winter camp provided to the students of Ghavis with an opportunity to learn new skills, have fun, and interact with other children from their community.The students also had the opportunity to learn about the local culture and traditions,The camp was a valuable experience for the students.

About Ghavis

Let's Know About Ghavis

Ghavis is a small stream valley in Gilgit-Baltistan(Kharmang), Nearly 800 to 1000 peoples living in Ghavis Valley Anjumanbabulhawaej is an organization who works for Ghavis. Ghavis is situated nearby to the localities Palpaldo and Thang
